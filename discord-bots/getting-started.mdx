# Getting Started with Discord Bot Hosting

Follow these steps to launch your first Discord bot on Cybrance.nl:

1. Choose your bot language (Python or Node.js)
2. Use one of our templates to get started quickly
3. Push your code to GitHub
4. Connect your repository in the Cybrance dashboard
5. Deploy and manage your bot with ease
