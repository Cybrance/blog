# How to Host a Replit Bot on Cytric

Move your bot from Replit to Cytric for better reliability and features.

**Steps:**

1. Export your bot code from Replit.
2. Push it to a GitHub repository.
3. Connect your repo in the Cytric dashboard.
4. Deploy and manage your bot with Cytric's hosting solution.

**Tip:** Cytric offers more control, uptime, and scalability than Replit.
